# Browse Projects

In this section, you will find a list of open-source projects that you can contribute to as a mentee.
Our project selection includes a mix of ALiAS's homegrown projects and other renowned open-source projects.
Each project is carefully curated to provide a unique learning experience and an opportunity for mentees to make meaningful contributions.
You can browse through the projects, explore their descriptions, and choose the ones that align with your interests and skills.
